Vegan Brownies

Intro

This is a dense, yet somewhat spongy vegan basic vanilla cake. It can be topped with a basic vegan chocolate or vegan vanilla buttercream frosting. A nice, easy, basic, homemade favorite that doesn't taste like a box mix and can be used as a tasty vegan birthday cake! Cool completely before topping with your favorite vegan buttercream frosting.



Ingredients

  • cup plain soy milk
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/4cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt/li>
  • 1/2tcup canola oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract

Directions


  1. Stir soy milk and vinegar together in a large glass measuring cup

  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.

  3. Briskly stir canola oil, water, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and almond extract into soy milk mixture using a fork. Stir soy milk mixture into flour mixture until batter is lump-free. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes.
